## Section Organization

The page presents examples across five main skill areas. **Note:** the "Maîtrise des structures de la langue" (Language Structure / Grammar) section appears in the page but is **not part of TCF Canada** — it is a TCF Tout Public / TCF IRN component only. Listed here for completeness.

### 1. Listening Comprehension (Compréhension orale)

Seven example types provided:

- **Examples 1-2:** Image matching with 4 multiple-choice responses
- **Examples 3-4:** Question-response matching (4 options)
- **Examples 5-7:** Document listening with follow-up questions

Audio passage types observed in samples:

- Conversational exchange (e.g., payment-methods negotiation)
- Workplace dialogue (e.g., scheduling appointments)
- Informational monologue (e.g., geopolitical research organization description)

### 2. Language Structure (Maîtrise des structures de la langue) — **NOT in TCF Canada**

Three grammar/vocabulary examples (8-10):

- Verb choice (apporter / emporter / emmener / ramener)
- Relative pronoun selection (que / lequel / auquel / duquel)
- Imperative form conjugation

This entire section exists only in TCF Tout Public, TCF IRN, etc. — confirmed against `landing-2026-05-10.md` which lists only 4 mandatory tests for TCF Canada.
